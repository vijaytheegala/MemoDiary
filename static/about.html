System Overview

Product Guide & Specs

A technical and functional breakdown of how MemoDiary works, what it remembers, and how to use it effectively.

Core Capabilities

Atomic Memory

Unlike standard chatbots, MemoDiary extracts "atomic facts" (entities, preferences, relationships) from every conversation and indexes them into a long-term vector relationship graph. It remembers details you mentioned weeks ago.

Voice-First Engine

Built with a custom Voice Activity Detection (VAD) layer. It detects natural pauses, processes speech in real-time, and responds with a streaming neural voice that feels human, not robotic.

Proactive Analysis

Every Monday, the system runs a background "Weekly Recap" routine. It analyzes your mood trends, topics discussed, and key memories from the past 7 days to give you a consolidated summary.

Session Isolation

Data architecture is built on strict session isolation. Your memories are keyed to a unique, anonymous token generated on your device. No cross-user data sharing.

Smart Synthesis

Connects specific details (like "feeling tired") with broader patterns (like "health trends") to provide insights you might miss yourself.

Adaptive Tone

The AI matches your energy. Whether you need a serious listener for deep reflection or a cheerful companion for casual chats, it adjusts automatically.

Interface Controls

The UI is designed for minimalism (Focus Mode). Here is how the key controls function:

Microphone: Tap to toggle listening. Auto-sends on silence.
Mute Toggle: Disables TTS (Text-to-Speech) output for silent reading.
Profile: View your active Session ID (Click to copy).

Usage & Prompt Guide

MemoDiary understands natural language, but here are specific ways to leverage its memory:

Recall "What did I say about my interview last week?"
Pattern Finding "Have I been talking about feeling tired a lot recently?"
Summarization "Summarize my diary entries from yesterday."
Fact Check "What is the name of the book I mentioned reading?"
